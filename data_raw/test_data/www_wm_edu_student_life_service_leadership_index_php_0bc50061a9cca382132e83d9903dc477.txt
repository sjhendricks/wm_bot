<p>At William &amp; Mary, service and leadership are central to our <a href="https://www.wm.edu/about/administration/vision-mission-values/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">mission</a> of cultivating responsible, engaged citizens.</p>
<div>Through the offices of <a href="https://www.wm.edu/offices/cce/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Civic &amp; Community Engagement</a>, <a href="https://www.wm.edu/offices/studentleadershipdevelopment/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Student Leadership Development </a>and many other organizations on and off campus, students are encouraged to actively contribute to society and develop skills that will enable them to make a positive impact in their communities. How will you get involved?</div>
